About Doggo

Doggo is a simple yet versatile Discord bot to assist you in server management. Making professional and high-quality Discord servers takes lots of time and effort. Hours of creating roles, channels, and informational texts. But what if you could do all that in less than 30 seconds? Introducing: Doggo. Doggo is a very simple yet powerful Discord bot built to assist in creating and maintaining Discord servers. With tools such as channel and role creation, as well as announcements and informational text templates, making a Discord server has never been easier. Additionally, Doggo provides moderator commands to help ensure the safety of your servers. Fun commands and additions such as leveling, currency, image manipulation, and more will help keep your server members engaged and active. With incredibly fast speed and performance, what's there not to be excited for?

Doggo has many unique and useful features available for use:

  • Server Setup (channels, categories, etc.)
  • Moderation features (warn, kick, ban, mod-logs, etc.)
  • Informational Texts (rules, announcements, welcome, etc.)
  • Currency and Leveling System
  • User Informational Cards
  • Image Manipulation
  • And many more useful commands!

Doggo Premium

Doggo Premium  is much more feature-packed and exclusive than the free version of Doggo. Since hosting Doggo isn't free, we've created a premium version with exclusive features that can be purchased. When obtained, you'll gain access to our exclusive donator-only commands, as well as recognition in our official Discord server. Your support is greatly appreciated!
